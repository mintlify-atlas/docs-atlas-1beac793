HaishinKit is a Swift library for building live streaming applications on Apple platforms. It provides a unified API for publishing and playing back streams over RTMP, SRT, WHIP/WHEP, and MoQT protocols, with built-in support for multi-camera capture, video mixing, screen capture, and audio mixing — all using Swift's modern async/await concurrency model.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install HaishinKit">
    Add the Swift package to your Xcode project from `https://github.com/HaishinKit/HaishinKit.swift`, selecting the modules you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure permissions">
    Add `NSCameraUsageDescription` and `NSMicrophoneUsageDescription` to your `Info.plist`, and set up `AVAudioSession` on iOS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a MediaMixer">
    Attach your camera and microphone devices to a `MediaMixer` instance and call `startRunning()`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect and publish">
    Create an `RTMPConnection` or `SRTConnection`, connect to your server, and publish your stream.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  HaishinKit requires iOS 15+, macOS 12+, tvOS 15+, or visionOS 1.0+, and Swift 6.0 with Xcode 16.4 or later.
</Note>
